THE PROGRAM

Tangible care.
Real connection.
A reason to hold on.

REACH is Esther Funds Foundation’s curated care-package program for college students. Through verified campus ambassadors, it delivers practical essentials, encouragement, and resource connections when students need them most.

THE FIVE PILLARS

R.E.A.C.H. is the route.

R

Reach Out

Ask early. Help is available, and it is okay to reach for it before a challenge becomes a crisis.

E

Engage Your Community

College is also people. Connect to peers, campus offices, ambassadors, and community support.

A

Access Resources

Scholarships, financial aid, SNAP/EBT, food pantries, tutoring, and support routes exist to be used.

C + H

Care & Hold On

Mental health matters. Every completed semester is a victory. In a crisis, call 911 or call/text 988.

WHAT’S INSIDE

A box with a route beyond it.

  • Food and hydration items
  • Hygiene and laundry essentials
  • Academic tools and encouragement
  • REACH merch and practical supports
  • Printed resources: SNAP/EBT, campus pantry, 988, financial aid, scholarships, and study tips

Final contents are subject to sourcing and program decisions.

HOW DISTRIBUTION WORKS

Ambassador-led. Student-centered.

  1. Student submits a request.
  2. EFF reviews the request.
  3. Approved students receive a notification.
  4. A verified campus ambassador coordinates in-person pickup.
  5. Students can choose to share an impact photo only with consent.
