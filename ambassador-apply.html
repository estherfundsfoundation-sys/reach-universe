CAMPUS LEADERSHIP ROLE

Become the
reason someone
keeps going.

REACH Ambassadors are verified campus leaders who help distribute approved REACH Boxes in person, share student resources, lead workshops, connect students to support, and represent EFF’s mission with care.

What you’ll do: coordinate campus pickup, host or support REACH programming, share the Action Hub, maintain respectful boundaries, protect privacy, complete training, and report impact.

What you won’t do: promise aid, act as a counselor, handle a student crisis alone, or share private student information.
